About Us

At Hatle Maintenance we spacialize in meeting the construction and renovation requirements of a diverse range of commercial, retail, and residential clients. We are the preferred contractor panel for multiple companies and property management companies.

Our dedicated team of construction, renovation and restoration experts have accumulated a wealth of experience in the construction of modern homes. We have Quantity Surveyor’s and Project Managers to forecast cost and duration of your project prior to commencement. Saving you both time and money. Whatever project we are working on, we believe in applying the highest possible standards to each, ensuring that we remain within budget and on time with the highest possible attention to detail. If you have any maintenance, renovation or fencing needs in the Western Cape, please don’t hesitate to contact us. Our team will gladly assist you from start-to-end.
